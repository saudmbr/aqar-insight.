Redesign the "Market Insights / مؤشرات السوق العقاري" section into a premium, decision-driven analytics dashboard.

Goal:
Transform this section from basic numbers into a powerful real estate decision tool that helps users understand the market, evaluate prices, and identify opportunities.

IMPORTANT:
- Current numbers are placeholders → redesign structure, not values
- Remove any repetitive or meaningless metrics
- Focus only on high-impact, decision-relevant insights

----------------------------------

1) STRUCTURE (REBUILD THE SECTION)

Divide the section into 4 clear blocks:

A) Market Overview (نظرة عامة)
B) Price Intelligence (تحليل الأسعار)
C) Supply & Demand (العرض والطلب)
D) Opportunities & Insights (الفرص والتوصيات)

----------------------------------

2) BLOCK A – MARKET OVERVIEW

Keep only 3–4 strong KPIs:

- Average Property Price
- Median Price
- Average Price per sqm
- Total Active Listings

Each KPI must include:
- value
- change vs last 30 days (↑ ↓ %)
- small insight text

Example:
"↑ 12% خلال آخر 30 يوم – زيادة في الطلب"

----------------------------------

3) BLOCK B – PRICE INTELLIGENCE (MOST IMPORTANT)

Add decision-based metrics:

- Fair Value Indicator:
  - Cheap / Fair / Expensive
  - based on market average

- Price vs Area Average:
  - "أعلى من متوسط الحي بـ 15%"
  - "أقل من السوق بـ 8%"

- Price Trend:
  - Rising / Stable / Falling

- Price Distribution:
  - Low / Average / High range

----------------------------------

4) BLOCK C – SUPPLY & DEMAND

Replace weak counters with meaningful insights:

- New listings (last 7 days)
- New listings (last 30 days)
- Supply trend:
  - increasing / decreasing

- Demand indicator:
  - High / Medium / Low

- Sale vs Rent ratio

----------------------------------

5) BLOCK D – OPPORTUNITIES & INSIGHTS (KEY DIFFERENTIATOR)

This is the most important part:

Add a smart insight panel:

- "فرصة استثمار":
  - area with high growth

- "منطقة نشطة":
  - high demand

- "سعر مرتفع":
  - overpriced zone warning

- "سوق مستقر":
  - low volatility

Each insight must:
- be short
- actionable
- easy to understand
